GRAPHIC DESIGNS

MAGAZINE LAYOUT

An 8 magazine-spread created in adobe Indesign, which showcased all my work I’ve accomplished in the class of GBDA101. My approach was going for a professional and minimal layout. The pages use rigid shapes to contain content and I often uses the whole spread to flow content.

PROCESS

Understanding how the spacing would be translated onto the printed magazine was a challenge. I used hexagons, circles and squares which didn’t feel cohesive, so I just stuck with rigid types of shapes such as hexagons and rectangles. Also, it was difficult trying to match the colour palette with the images of my projects, thus having to work around it.

INITIALS LOGO DESIGN

The objective was creating a logo based on the initials of our given client. We are given a written client statement that describes their goals and personalities, to help create a meaningful logo design. The final logo design at first glance embodies the essence of elegance from the use of gold colour and a navy blue. The paintbrush is the main object of focus in the centre and the brush strokes as the waves. Below is the bottom of an anchor that connects with the paintbrush as a full anchor. This shows how art is a means to ground and express herself.
